# Terms and Conditions of Sale

## 1. Purpose

These terms and conditions govern the sale of tickets and services offered by the BAF Camp (Belgian Activism Festi-Camp).

## 2. Price and Payment

Tickets are based on a pay-what-you-can model. Payment is made online via the ticketing system. A ticket is considered valid upon receipt of full payment.

## 3. Refund Policy

- Any ticket may be refunded up to 7 days before the start of the event.
- After this date, no refund will be possible, except in the event of cancellation of the event by the organizers.
- Transaction fees are non-refundable.

## 4. Ticket Transfer

Tickets are transferable to another person. To transfer a ticket, please contact us at info@belgian-activism-festi-camp.be.

## 5. Event Cancellation

In the event of cancellation, the full purchase price of tickets will be refunded.

## 6. Liability

BAF Camp declines all responsibility in case of loss, theft, or damage to personal property of participants.

## 7. Contact

For any questions regarding these terms of sale, you can reach us at info@belgian-activism-festi-camp.be.
