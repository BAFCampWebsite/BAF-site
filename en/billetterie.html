How does the free price work?

💚

Solidarity rate

Are you going through a difficult time or are you a student, unemployed, without income? You are welcome for little or no contribution.

⚖️

Fair price

You can contribute up to what the camp actually costs you: accommodation, meals, activities. We publish our accounts transparently.

🌟

Support rate

Do you have the means to contribute beyond the actual rate? Your generosity allows others to come. Thank you, really!

📖

Open accounts

All camp budget details will be published transparently on the pageFinancing.
