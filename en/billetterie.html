How does the pay-what-you-can scheme work?

💚

Solidarity pricing

Are you going through a difficult time, or are you a student, unemployed, or without an income? You’re welcome to attend for little or no contribution.

⚖️

Fair price

You can contribute the amount that the camp actually costs you: accommodation, meals, activities. We publish our accounts transparently.

🌟

Support rate

Can you afford to contribute more than the actual cost? Your generosity enables others to come. Thank you so much!

📖

Open accounts

Full details of the camp budget will be published transparently on the Funding page.
