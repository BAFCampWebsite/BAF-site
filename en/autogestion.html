Some information on how we work

Our structure

The BAF Camp is a self-managed event. Its mode of organization is based on general coordination made up of several working groups on the different aspects necessary for its success. The organization of this camp is done in an attempt at horizontality. Decisions are made by consent and we advocate an anti-authoritarian attitude. The organizing group is made up of people learning together to create these kinds of events, so it is open to anyone. No skills are required.

Volunteering

On site, our objective is for this event to take place in the most horizontal and self-managed manner possible. This is why, in order to erase as much as possible the border between volunteers & “consumers” of the festival, ideally, all participants give a little of their time to make this festival happen. We calculated that if all volunteers work2 hours per day, then the festival should be able to operate in a self-managed manner. Furthermore, any task can be adapted if you have any disability. Don't hesitate to register yourself as a volunteer by filling out the form below. Otherwise, you can find tasks to do in real time on the site!

The camp will be set up together.We'll need help!We will have to set up marquees and stages, but also create the decor, imagine spaces and build infrastructure using pallets according to our desires! Lots of things remain to be invented so come and take part in the creation of this space from August 16! Registrations in the form below 👇.
