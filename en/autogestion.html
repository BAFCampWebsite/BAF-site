Self-management

Some information about how we work

Our structure

BAF Camp is a self-managed event. It is organised through a general coordination team comprising several working groups covering the various aspects necessary for its success. The camp is organised with a focus on horizontal decision-making. Decisions are made by consensus and we advocate an anti-authoritarian approach. The organising group consists of people learning together how to create this kind of event, so it is open to everyone. No specific skills are required.

The working groups

Here is the list of the various working groups; send an email by clicking on the button above if you’d like to join one of them.

💚

Care Working Group

To consider the practical and theoretical processes to be put in place to ensure that oppression does not occur during the organisation of the festival or whilst it is taking place.

🗣️

Communication Working Group

Managing social media, the website and external communications for the event.

🫶

Volunteers Working Group

Handling the logistics of volunteering at the camp site.

⚒️

Logistics Working Group

Arrange the necessary equipment for the festival as well as site logistics (layout of the various festival areas, managing arrivals and departures, etc.)

🎤

Programming Working Group

Proposing workshop themes for the festival programme and contacting the relevant groups/individuals.

💰

Money & Legal Working Group

Set a budget for the festival, contact donors, consider the festival’s legal protection and apply for the necessary permits.

🔄

Coordination Working Group

Supports the other working groups and ensures smooth communication between them.

Volunteering

On-site, our aim is for this event to run as horizontally and self-managed as possible. That’s why, to blur the line between volunteers and festival ‘attendees’ as much as possible, ideally, all participants give a little of their time to keep the festival running. We’ve calculated that if all volunteers work two hours a day , the festival should be able to run on a self-managed basis. Furthermore, any task can be adapted if you have a disability of any kind. Feel free to sign up as a volunteer now by filling in the form below. Alternatively, you’ll be able to find tasks to do in real time on the site!

The camp will be set up collectively. We’ll need help! We’ll need to put up marquees and stages, but also create the decor, design spaces and build infrastructure using pallets according to our ideas! There’s plenty left to invent, so come and help create this space from 16 August! Sign up using the form below 👇.
