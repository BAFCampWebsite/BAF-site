Practical

Know everything

Come to the place, sleep there and eat there—everything you need to come and stay at BAF Camp 2026.

🚧 Site under construction — complete and final information will be announced soon!

📍 How to get there

Access to the festival

🚆

Train

The site is 300 m walk from Grupont station

🚲

Bike

Possible to reach the place by bike. Parking and repair equipment on site.

🚗

Car

Direct access to the site by car possible at the address: Chem. de Lavaux 1, 6927 Tellin, Belgium. Free parking on site, carpooling encouraged.

🍴 Eat & drink

Restoration

Canteen arrangements have not yet been defined. If you are part of a collective that can help with equipment and know-how.Contact us!

⛺ Sleep on site

Camping

The campsite is open fromAugust 17 to 26, on a grassy area a stone's throw from the camp site.

A campsite exclusively for a chosen mix of MINT (Girl, Intersex, Non-binary and Trans) will be set up.

🗓️
Opening dates

Arrival possible from Sunday August 16 from 2 p.m. Departure no later than Tuesday August 26 at 12 p.m.

🧒 Little activists

The Baby Room

BAF Camp is also for families! A dedicated spacefor children between 4 and 16 years oldis planned on the site, so that parents can enjoy the conferences and workshops with peace of mind.

🌅
Morning slot

A niche of2am in the morningis planned to drop off the children at the Bambinerie, while they attend the program's conferences and workshops.

🌤️
Afternoon slot

A second slot of2 hours in the afternoonis offered to allow parents to fully enjoy the day.

🎨
Activities for toddlers

Outside of childcare slots, certain organized activities can be adapted for toddlers: games, creations, discoveries... BAF Camp is for everyone!

💬
Questions about the Bambinerie?

Do not hesitate to contact us atactivismcampbelgium@proton.mefor any questions regarding childcare.
