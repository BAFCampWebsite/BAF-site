Join us!

This camp will be the sum (or even more!) of the people who make it up. Come and tip the scales in our favour.
No prior skills or qualifications are required to help out. Arrangements will be made according to requests. Here are the 3 levels of involvement available:

🤩

Become a volunteer

Set aside some time during your stay to help ensure everything runs smoothly on site. If everyone gives 2 hours a day, all shifts will be covered!

Make a donation

Because we want to create an accessible space, there will be no restrictions on access other than the availability of space at the camp site.
However, despite the work of hundreds of volunteers, certain expenses remain and cannot be reduced.
Your donation, however small, allows us to remain independent and true to our values.
