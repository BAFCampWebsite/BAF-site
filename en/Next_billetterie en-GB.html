Pay what you can

So you can contribute what you can afford — even if it’s €0.

What is a pay-what-you-can, conscious price?

BAF Camp doesn’t operate on a fixed price. Everyone contributes according to their actual means — not just their salary, but their wealth, their privileges, their safety nets. The wealthiest and most privileged pay more, which allows others to attend.

If you really can’t afford to pay, it’s perfectly fine to come for free. Pay-what-you-can means exactly that — including the option to pay nothing. That’s the whole idea: those with more resources make up for those with less.

Pay-what-you-can doesn’t always work well — the privileged often tend to underpay. That’s why we invite you to think honestly about your situation before choosing your amount.
