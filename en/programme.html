✦ Program

How to fight together?

The program will revolve around this question.

🚧 Site under construction — the full program will be announced soon!

Let’s build the program together!

The camp will revolve around two concepts to try to shed light on the following questions:

"Together"": Locate ourselves and find our common points.
What do we have in common? How can we improve the inclusiveness of our struggles and make ourselves accessible? How can we make our disagreements visible and find points of agreement around which we can rally for mutual benefits? How can we not be oppressors of each other within the struggle (anti-racism, power dynamics, feminism, anti-validism)?

"The Struggle"": Visibility and mapping our struggles.
How can we support each other? What subjects are common and what are the places of struggle near us? What strengths and specificities can be mobilized? in what places? In what ways? Share our experiences and our ways of fighting, learn from each other (concepts, strategies and creative ideas of struggles). How can we create a powerful and effective collective logistical fabric for our struggles?

Discover the ideas that will liven up the camp this year. Common thread of the upcoming program.
