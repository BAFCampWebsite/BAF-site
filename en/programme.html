✦ Programme

How can we fight together?

The programme will revolve around this question.

🚧 Site under construction — the full programme will be announced shortly!

Let’s build the programme together!

The camp will be structured around two concepts to try to shed light on the following questions:

"Together" : Positioning ourselves and finding our common ground.
What do we have in common? How can we improve the inclusivity of our struggles and make ourselves accessible? How can we make our disagreements visible and find points of agreement around which to rally for mutual benefit? How can we avoid oppressing one another within the struggle (anti-racism, power dynamics, feminism, anti-ableism)?

“The Struggle ”: Making our struggles visible and mapping them out.
How can we support one another? What issues do we share, and where are the sites of struggle close to home? What strengths and specificities can be mobilised? Where? In what ways? Sharing our experiences and our ways of fighting, learning from one another (concepts, strategies and creative ideas for struggle). How can we create a powerful and effective collective logistical network for our struggles?

Discover the ideas that will drive the camp this year. A preview of the programme to come.
