✦ Finance & Transparency

Financing

How the BAF Camp is financed and the presentation of our budget in complete transparency.

🚧 Site under construction — complete and final information will be announced soon!

Funding sources

The camp is self-funded. The vast majority of the festival is funded by participants. The majority of financial sources will come from free-price ticket sales, as well as on-site purchases. For that :

Bring cash!
The distributors are far from the site.

Here's how we plan to achieve a sustainable financial balance.

🎟️

Ticketing

Ticket sales represent the festival's main source of income. Conscious and informed free pricing.

🍺

Bar & Restoration

Revenue from the bar contributes to the financial balance of the event. Those for meals will return to the canteen.

🔄

Benefits?

Once the festival breaks even, we will use the potential profit to pay the speakers. After that, all possible profits from the event will be used for current or future struggles.

Budget transparency

The festival accounts will be accessible to everyone. The results will be presented after the edition.

  • 2026 financial report available on request
  • Reinjection of profits into control projects
