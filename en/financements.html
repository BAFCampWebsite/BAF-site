✦ Finance & Transparency

Funding

How BAF Camp is funded and a full breakdown of our budget.

🚧 Site under construction — full and final details will be announced shortly!

Sources of funding

The camp is self-funded. The vast majority of the festival is funded by the participants. The main sources of funding will come from pay-what-you-can ticket sales, as well as on-site purchases. So:

Bring cash!
Cash machines are a long way from the site.

Here’s how we plan to achieve a sustainable financial balance.

🎟️

Ticket sales

Ticket sales are the festival’s main source of income. Conscious and informed pay-what-you-can pricing.

🍺

Bar & Catering

Revenue from the bar contributes to the event’s financial balance. Revenue from meals will go to the canteen.

🔄

Profits?

Once the festival has broken even, we will use any potential profit to pay the performers. After that, any remaining profits from the event will be used to support ongoing or future campaigns.

Budget transparency

The festival’s accounts will be accessible to everyone. The financial statement will be presented after the event.

  • 2026 financial statement available on request
  • Reinvestment of profits in activist projects
